About Us

Our Mission

At Boomerang, our mission is to bring joy and satisfaction to our customers through exceptional ice cream made from the highest-quality ingredients. We are dedicated to crafting unique and delicious flavors that create delightful moments with every scoop. Whether through our innovative recipes, our friendly customer service, or our commitment to environmental sustainability, we aim to exceed your expectations and make every visit memorable.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by ice cream enthusiasts Jane and Mark Roberts, Boomerang began with a simple idea: to create artisanal ice cream that brings a smile to your face. Starting from a small shop in Denver, Colorado, what began as a local favorite quickly grew into a beloved brand known for its rich flavors and dedication to quality. Over the years, Boomerang has expanded across the country, and from 2010 to 2024, we have proudly served countless customers with our signature blends. Our passion for ice cream and commitment to excellence continue to drive us forward, making every day a celebration of sweet, creamy delight.

Our Values

Quality

We use the finest ingredients and uphold the highest standards to ensure every scoop is of exceptional quality.

We continuously explore new flavors and techniques to bring exciting and unique offerings to our customers.

Customer Satisfaction

Your happiness is our top priority. We strive to create unforgettable experiences and build lasting relationships with our customers.

Favourites

Black Current

Black currant ice cream offers a unique and tantalizing flavor experience, distinguished by the rich, tart essence of black currants. Known for their deep purple color and intense, tangy taste, black currants are a delightful choice for an ice cream that is both refreshing and exotic. The ice cream's base is typically creamy and smooth, providing a perfect balance to the robust flavor of the black currants. Each spoonful delivers a burst of fruity vibrancy, complemented by subtle notes of sweetness. Ideal for those who appreciate a sophisticated twist on traditional flavors, black currant ice cream is often enhanced with additional ingredients like a hint of vanilla or a touch of citrus to elevate its taste. Whether enjoyed on a warm day or as a novel dessert option, black currant ice cream stands out for its distinctive and delicious profile.

Brownie with Vanilla Ice Cream

A classic and indulgent treat, the combination of a rich, fudgy brownie with creamy vanilla ice cream is a perfect marriage of flavors and textures. The brownie, with its dense, chocolatey goodness and gooey center, provides a decadent contrast to the smooth, cool vanilla ice cream. The warm brownie melts slightly as it meets the ice cream, creating a delightful fusion of temperatures and a deliciously contrasting bite. The vanilla ice cream, with its creamy, sweet flavor, complements the deep, intense chocolate notes of the brownie, while its subtle vanilla essence adds an extra layer of depth. This pairing is not only visually appealing but also a sensory delight, offering a satisfying blend of warm and cold, soft and chewy, sweet and slightly bitter. Ideal for a comforting dessert.

Strawberry

Strawberries are a beloved fruit renowned for their vibrant red color, sweet flavor, and juicy texture. These succulent berries are not only visually appealing with their bright hue and delicate seeds but also offer a refreshing burst of natural sweetness with just a hint of tartness. Each bite delivers a satisfying blend of juicy and tender flesh, making strawberries a delightful addition to a variety of dishes. Whether enjoyed fresh on their own, added to salads, blended into smoothies, or featured in desserts like tarts and ice creams, strawberries bring a touch of summer to any occasion. Rich in vitamins, antioxidants, and fiber, they not only please the palate but also contribute to a healthy diet. Their versatility and irresistible taste make strawberries a perennial favorite among fruit lovers.

Chocolate

Chocolate cone ice cream is a delightful treat that combines the creamy richness of chocolate ice cream with the crisp, crunchy texture of a classic waffle cone. The ice cream itself is a luscious, velvety blend of cocoa, delivering a deep, satisfying chocolate flavor that melts smoothly on the tongue. Each bite is a harmonious experience, where the decadent chocolate ice cream contrasts beautifully with the sweet, slightly toasted notes of the cone. The waffle cone adds a satisfying crunch, enhancing the overall texture and creating a perfect balance between the creamy and crispy elements. Ideal for both chocolate enthusiasts and casual ice cream lovers, chocolate cone ice cream offers a timeless indulgence that is as visually appealing as it is delicious, making it a standout choice for a refreshing and enjoyable dessert.
